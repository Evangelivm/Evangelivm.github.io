Scenarios

  • If you need to travel with your family, you need a reliable and easily accessible tool.
  • When you want to go to the local stadium, you need to know what clothes you need to wear, since where you live the climate is varied.
  • Sometimes you need to go to another city for business, so you need to know what kind of clothes you need to bring in your luggage.
  • A while ago he had a bad experience when his car broke down due to bad weather, so he needs to know if he will use a bus or his car.
  • He is a fan of analog photography, so he has to keep an eye on the weather to know if it is the ideal time to take pictures.
